Australian man 'nearly fainted' when he realized he paid $700,000 for 'half a house'
To the news
It's not a freestanding house, it's not a duplex, it's half a house. And it looks embarrassing'

An Australian man says he nearly fainted when work finally finished on his custom-built home and he realized he really only had “half a house”.Bishnu Aryal, who moved to Sydney from Nepal, reportedly saved for 10 years to buy a plot of land in Edmonson Park for AUS$398,950 ($388,400). In 2016, he hired Zac Homes to build him a detached home for AUS$322,400 ($313,900), news.com.au reports.
